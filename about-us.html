About

The Group

YIJIA, founded on 28 March 2004, has over 30 subsidiaries around the world, with businesses spanning across over 50 countries and regions worldwide in a wide range of fields including cosmetics, jewelry, travel and tourism, agricultural ecology and production, e-commerce, investment, real estate, finance management, education, and high-tech products, etc.

YIJIA features three major product lines, namely, MAIONE for skin care, MIRIKEL for dietary supplements and Vii for daily necessities. Adopting plants as raw materials, all products are produced based on the cruelty-free principle. YIJIA is furnished with a professional team specializing in research and development, production, sales and service; it is also in possession of a Dalbergia odorifera tree plantation and production base that benefits from unique geographical advantages

While devoting to run business and help others, YIJIA has been committed to charity and giving back to the society. The Group has donated over USD $10 million to people and organizations residing in various countries and regions including China, Singapore, Hong Kong, Taiwan, Malaysia, Canada, Australia, the U.S., Thailand, Indonesia, and Macao.

Since its foundation, YIJIA North America has opened four branch offices. With an advanced business model, well-established system, high-quality products, and excellent service, we have won a great reputation and attracted numerous partners to join us. In addition, with the establishment of YIJIA Charity Foundation, we hope to encourage our local community to participate in our ongoing charitable activities.
